def random_players(n_p):
    players = []
    for p in range(0, n_p):
        pos = [random.randrange(1, width), random.randrange(1, height)]
        player = Player(pos, p)
        players.append(player)
    return(players)

Player()构造函数需要一个位置和一个 ID。ID 是它在玩家列表中的索引位置。
